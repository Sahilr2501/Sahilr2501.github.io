CRM for Growatt InfoSystem

Creative solutions for UI/UX, logo, and more worldwide

Overview

Growatt InfoSystem provies a wide range of graprhic design services to clients across the globe. From logos and banners to brochures and labels, we deliver creative solutions tailored to meet your needs.

Key Features

    Admin Side:

  • Admin Authentication & Security: Secure access with JWT-based authentication and role-based control.
  • Admin Interactive Dashboard: Centralized panel for managing content, analytics, and user data.
  • Portfolio Management: Add, update, and delete project showcases with ease.
  • Service Pricing Management: Manage service packages, pricing tiers, and feature highlights.
  • Career & Job Application Handling: Post job openings and manage candidate submissions with resume uploads.
  • Team Management: Add and showcase team members and their milestone stories.
  • Customer & Testimonial Management: Display client feedback and logos dynamically across the platform.
  • Contact Information Customization: Update company contact details, social links, and embedded map easily.
  • Admin Account Management: Manage admin profiles, reset passwords, and control access levels.

    • User Side:

  • Interactive Portfolio Showcase: Engaging gallery of past projects with categories and project details.
  • Service Management: Present all design and development services with detailed descriptions.
  • Client Testimonials: Highlight customer reviews and success stories for brand trust.
  • Contact & Inquiry Handling: Receive and manage user messages via contact form and database logging.
  • Career & Job Application System: Career page with position listings and secure application submission.
  • AI Chat System: Smart chatbot for FAQs, service queries, and instant engagement.
  • Responsive Design: Mobile-first design using ReactJS and Tailwind CSS for seamless user experience on all devices.

Technologies Used

HTML5 CSS3 Tailwind CSS JavaScript React.js Node.js Express.js MongoDB

Purpose

The purpose of Growatt InfoSystem is to deliver visually compelling and user-friendly design solutions that enhance brand identity and improve user interaction. Our goal is to help businesses stand out in a competitive digital landscape through innovative UI/UX and graphic design services.
